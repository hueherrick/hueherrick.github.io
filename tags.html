Morro Bay California

Morro Bay is one of my favorite desitinations along the coast of California.

Things to do in Morro Bay California

  1. Grab a bite at Giovanni's Fish Market, and Galley
  2. Surf on the Northern Side of Morro Rock
  3. Kayak in the harbor
  4. Watch the otters on the south side of the rock

Places to eat in Morro Bay California

  1. Giovanni's Fish Market, and Galley
  2. Great American Fish Company
  3. House of Juju
  4. Dutchman's Seafood Grill
